Discounts

Customers (employers or employees) can select from a range of courses available.

They can select more than one course. The more courses the customers select the more discount they receive.

  • One course – no discount
  • Two courses – 5% discount
  • Three courses – 10% discount
  • More than three courses – 15% discount
